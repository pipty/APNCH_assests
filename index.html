2nd Annual Conference of the

Climate Change and Human Health in the Asia-Pacific
Exposure Assessment, Current Impacts, Future Risks, and Health Benefits of Climate Actions

Dear colleagues, 

We are pleased to announce the 2nd Annual Conference of the Asia Pacific Network for Climate Health, to be held in Melbourne, Australia, from 30 September to 4 October 2026.


Conference Details


This international conference will bring together researchers, clinicians, policymakers, and early-career scientists working at the intersection of climate change, environmental exposures, and human health, with a particular focus on Asia and the Asia-Pacific region.

The conference will provide a platform to share new evidence, methodological innovations, and policy-relevant insights across the full pathway from exposure assessment and current health impacts to future risks and mitigation strategies.

Call for Abstracts

HOW TO SUBMIT

  1. Please prepare your abstract using the APNCH abstract template (Download:  APNCH_Abstract_Template.docx), and submit it via email.
  2. All abstracts must be written in English.
  3. All abstracts must be submitted before 15th April, 2026.
  4. Send your abstract as an email attachment to:  apnch_abstract@monash.edu
